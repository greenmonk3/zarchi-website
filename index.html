History Lesson

Humans have always been fascinated by combat. In a philosophical perspective, it's in our nature. We used to decide most things by combat and to an extent we still do: Evident from wars and fighting sports. So I couldn't help ponder how I would fend against a chimpanzee. Now that you know my excuse for this highly controversial debate, I'll present the sides.

Me

Analysis

Coming from Arizona… It's me. Now at first glance, you may think I have no chance. But keep in mind that I'm human, the top of the food chain. Since I technically made this debate, I can do whatever I want. Thus, you should keep in mind I have both the power of weapons and the power of plot armor (I can rig the results).

Chimpanzee

Analysis

Chimpanzees are among the smartest animals on earth, which makes them a worthy rival to a human. They can use tools, which in the animal kingdom is comparable to a superpower. They are amazing swimmers and fighters. They share almost all of our DNA, which may prove to be a disadvantage, since who knows…genetics could make them feel bad for us humans. Although, probably the most important thing to note is that chimpanzees are native to africa, which coincidentally requires a rather expensive plane ticket.

So Who do you choose?
